# ChatFaker - HOW TO Setup in App Store Connect

## Step 1: Create New App

1. Go to [App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com)
2. Login with your Apple Developer account
3. Click **"My Apps"** → **"+"** → **"New App"**
4. Fill in the details:

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Platforms | iOS |
| Name | **ChatFaker** |
| Primary Language | English |
| Bundle ID | com.ggsheng.FakeChat |
| SKU | chatfaker-001 |

## Step 2: App Information

- **Age Rating**: 12+
- **Category**: Entertainment / Social Networking
- **Privacy Policy URL**: https://lauer3912.github.io/ios-FakeChat/docs/PrivacyPolicy.html

## Step 3: Pricing

- **Price Schedule**: Free with ads, or set your preferred pricing

## Step 4: Prepare Screenshots

Required sizes:
- iPhone 6.9" (1290x2796): 5 screenshots
- iPhone 6.5" (1284x2778): 5 screenshots
- iPad 12.9" (2048x2732): 3 screenshots

## Step 5: Upload Build

1. After creating the app record, upload the build via Xcode
2. Wait for build to appear in "Build" section
3. Select the build for submission

## Step 6: Submit for Review

- **Review Notes**:
