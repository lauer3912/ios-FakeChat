Privacy Policy for ChatFaker

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

ChatFaker is designed with your privacy in mind. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal data is collected
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No account or login required
  • No social features that collect data
  • No data is sent to external servers

Local Data Storage

All data created within ChatFaker (conversations, settings, history) is stored locally on your device. This data is never uploaded or shared with anyone.

Photos & Camera Access

If you grant permission, ChatFaker can access your photo library and camera to:

  • Select custom avatars for contacts
  • Choose custom wallpapers
  • Save generated screenshots to your photo library

These images are processed locally and are never uploaded or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

ChatFaker does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection tools.

Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under 13. However, we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page.
